abstract: |-
  We propose **Fun-CineForge**, a unified solution for zero-shot movie dubbing in diverse cinematic scenes, comprising (1) an end-to-end production pipeline for large-scale multimodal dubbing data and (2) a multimodal large model purpose-built for diverse cinematic scenarios. Building on this pipeline, we construct and open-source **CineDub**, the first dedicated movie-dubbing dataset, from scratch. Across monologue, narration, dialogue, and complex multi-speaker scenes, Fun-CineForge surpasses state-of-the-art methods on speech quality, temporal alignment, lip-sync accuracy, timbre similarity, and instruction following. To our knowledge, Fun-CineForge is the first dubbing model that relies on the **temporal modality** for time alignment and supports long-video dubbing across diverse cinematic scenarios. Fun-CineForge has been open-sourced in Alibaba Tongyi's official speech repository (10K+ downloads), with demos released on HuggingFace and ModelScope.
