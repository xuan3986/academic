abstract: |-
  Conventional emotional TTS relies on discrete emotion labels, failing to capture the complexity and continuity of human emotional expression. **UDDETTS** unifies discrete and dimensional emotion modeling: it introduces the interpretable **Arousal–Dominance–Valence (ADV)** space and supports control via either discrete labels or non-linearly quantized ADV values. By disentangling emotion along three psychologically meaningful dimensions, UDDETTS enables fine-grained linear 3-D emotion control and significantly outperforms baselines in naturalness and emotion-control accuracy. Work conducted under an NSFC Joint-Fund project.
