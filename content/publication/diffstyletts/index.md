abstract: |-
  **DiffStyleTTS** is a multi-speaker acoustic model based on a conditional diffusion module and an improved classifier-free guidance strategy. It hierarchically models speech prosodic features and steers different prosodic styles to guide prosody prediction. Experiments show that DiffStyleTTS outperforms representative baselines in prosody-expressiveness control, achieves higher naturalness, and yields superior performance in prosody transfer and prosody control — combining diversity and controllability of speech prosody. Joint work with iFLYTEK and China Mobile Research.
