abstract: |-
  We present **DiffStyleTTS**, a multi-speaker acoustic model based on a **conditional diffusion module** and an **improved classifier-free guidance** strategy. DiffStyleTTS hierarchically models speech prosodic features and controls different prosodic styles to guide prosody prediction. Experiments show that our method outperforms representative baselines in **prosody-expressiveness control**, achieves higher **naturalness**, and yields superior performance in **prosody transfer** and **prosody control**, simultaneously achieving diversity and controllability of speech prosody.
