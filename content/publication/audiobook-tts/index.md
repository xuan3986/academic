abstract: |-
  To address the **listener fatigue** caused by audiobook narration that relies on a small set of explicit, discrete emotion categories, we construct a **multi-modal continuous emotional space** to escape the limits of explicit emotion taxonomies. Building on a **T5** large model and **contrastive learning**, we propose a cross-modal supervised text-emotion-perception module, which significantly improves emotional similarity between the synthesized audiobook speech and its source text and context.
